Expert Chess







How to play Expert Chess

The game will start on the chess board. You begin.
You are the white site. Click one of your tokens and possible moves will be indicated by green lights. The usual chess rules apply here, but if you or your enemy artificial intelligence step on a red field a minigame will begin!



Inside the minigames you can use the mouse and your keyboard to move around, instructions will be displayed before any minigame.

Have Fun!
